Elon Reeves Musk

Managing Director of Tesla Motors

Here's a time line of Elon Musks life:

  • 1971 - Born in pretoria south africa.
  • 1995 - Co-founded web software company Zip2
  • 1999 - Compaq acquired Zip2 for $307 million.
  • 2000 - Co-founded X.com, an online payment company.
  • 2001 - X.com renamed to PayPal after the merger with Confinity
  • 2002 - eBay acquired PayPal for $1.5 billion.
  • 2002 - SpaceX successfully launched its first privately-funded rocket, Falcon 1.
  • 2004 - Invested in Tesla Motors (now Tesla, Inc.).
  • 2006 - Tesla Motors released its first electric car, the Roadster.
  • 2008 - SpaceX won a NASA contract to transport cargo to the International Space Station.
  • 2012 - Tesla released its Model S electric sedan
  • 2013 -Launched the concept of the Hyperloop high-speed transportation system.
  • 2015 - SpaceX successfully landed the first stage of its Falcon 9 rocket vertically
  • 2015 - Founded Neuralink, a neurotechnology company focused on brain-computer interfaces
  • 2016 - Co-founded OpenAI, an artificial intelligence research organization.
  • 2017 -Launched The Boring Company, focused on building tunnels for transportation systems
  • 2018 -SpaceX launched Falcon Heavy, the most powerful operational rocket in the world.
  • 2020 -Tesla became the world's most valuable automaker by market capitalization.
  • 2020 - SpaceX successfully launched and docked astronauts to the International Space Station.
  • 2021 -Tesla invested in Bitcoin and announced acceptance of the cryptocurrency as payment.
